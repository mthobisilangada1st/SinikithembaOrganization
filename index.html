Serving the KwaDukuza Community

Sinikithemba Organization is a non-profit organisation registered in 2012, based at Groutville Ward 10. We are committed to improving the quality of life of orphans and vulnerable children, individuals, and families, including those affected and infected by HIV/AIDS.

Our organization addresses social, emotional, and behavioural challenges through programmes that foster psychosocial well-being, positive behaviour change, educational support, and community awareness.

Our Services

1. Psychosocial Support
We provide counselling, emotional support, and life-skills development to help individuals cope with social challenges. Our services aim to strengthen mental well-being, self-esteem, and resilience among children, adolescents, and caregivers.

2. Behavioural Change Programmes
Our behavioural change programmes promote positive attitudes and healthy lifestyles. These initiatives focus on reducing risky behaviours, improving decision-making, encouraging adherence to treatment, and fostering responsible social behaviour through education and mentorship.

3. After-School Programmes
We implement structured after-school programmes that offer academic support, mentorship, recreational activities, and life-skills training. These programmes provide a safe and supportive environment that enhances learning outcomes and personal development for vulnerable children and youth.

4. Awareness Programmes
We conduct community awareness initiatives to increase knowledge on HIV/AIDS, child protection, health, and social inclusion. Our awareness activities aim to reduce stigma and discrimination, promote prevention, and strengthen community participation in supporting vulnerable populations.
